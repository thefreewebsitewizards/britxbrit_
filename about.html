Meet The Woman Behind The Mask

Hey there! I'm so glad you're here! As a fashion influencer based in America, I love sharing my journey and the unique styles I embrace. For over 4 years now, I have been sharing with the world my passion for Techwear. Whether it’s showing off your new found style at specific events or simply enjoying a day out, I believe fashion should be fun and personal. So let's inspire each other to dress boldly and express ourselves!

I am half Mexican half Caucasian yet I don’t know a bit of Spanish. I have 4 siblings and yes, they’re all girls! I live happily with my lovely partner and my beloved cat Meeko. You’ll see them pop up every now and then on either my photoshoots, videos, or my stories.

I started out my career from my passion for Techwear. How did I get into that exactly? Well, it all started from a days scrolling on my Youtube’s homepage in 2018 when i noticed this music video “Heart Attack” from the musician himself, Scarlxrd. I then got into his music then from there, his style.

I started out trying out the style for fun. I made fun little outfit posts on my old Instagram. I noticed other pages that also were into Techwear, Darkwear, and alternative fashion who posted more professional photos on their page. I saw some even collaborating with pretty expensive fashion brands. I then thought to myself, “Well if they’re making money off something they love doing, I might as well work my way up into that career path. Fashion brings me joy so I’m glad this is what I chose.

I still may be a micro influencer with only a couple thousand followers supporting me at the moment, but that doesn’t mean I’m stopping there. Remember, it’s all about being yourself. There are countless fashion sources out there, but the magic happens when you add your own twist to the trends. You guys are the ones who keep me going. You guys are the ones who bring me motivation and you guys are the ones helping me through it all with your brilliant resources. We grow as a family!
